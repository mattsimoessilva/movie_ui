Introducing Movie Application - Your Ultimate Film Database

Movie Application is designed to streamline movie management, allowing users to effortlessly add movies, people, and roles to build a comprehensive film database. Whether you're organizing a personal collection or managing industry-related data, our app makes it easy to keep track of essential details.

  • Movies - Add new films with relevant metadata, including titles, budget, box office, and more.
  • People - Keep a record of actors, directors, writers, and other film professionals involved in each project.
  • Roles - Assign roles to individuals, ensuring clarity in their participation.

With an intuitive interface and powerful organization features, Movie Application simplifies movie tracking while keeping everything structured and accessible.
